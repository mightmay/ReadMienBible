1 Ziouv gorngv mbuox Mose, 2 “Zih hlaax saeng-yietv wuov hnoi, oix zuqc corng Wuic Buangh nyei Ndopv-Liuh. 3 Zorqv Laengz Ngaengc Waac Faang an jienv ndopv-liuh gu'nyuoz aengx zorqv ndie-mbaeqc torngv jienv. 4 Dorh dieh bieqc daaih don jienv yaac zorqv dieh nyei jaa-sic an jienv dieh gu'nguaaic. Dorh dang-jaax bieqc daaih aengx zorqv dang-zaanv an jienv ninh mbuo nyei dorngx. 5 Zorqv buov hung nyei jiem-dieh don jienv Laengz Ngaengc Waac Faang nza'hmien maengx, aengx zorqv mbaeqc ndopv-liuh nyei gaengh wuov kuaaiv ndie mbaeqc jienv.

6 “Zorqv buov ziec nyei ga'naaiv nyei ziec-dorngh don jienv doix Wuic Buangh Ndopv-Liuh nyei gaengh ndaangc. 7 Zorqv bunh don jienv ziec-dorngh caux ndopv-liuh mbu'ndongx, aengx dapv jienv wuom. 8 Corng laatc weih jienv biei bung ndopv-liuh, aengx zorqv ndie mbaeqc jienv laatc gaengh.

9 “Zorqv orn mienh nyei youh dox ndopv-liuh caux ndopv-liuh nyei yietc zungv jaa-sic fungx bun Ziouv. Nyungc-nyungc ziouc cing-nzengc mi'aqv. 10 Aengx longc youh dox ziec-dorngh, se buov ziec nyei ga'naaiv nyei ziec-dorngh, caux ziec-dorngh nyei yietc zungv jaa-sic, ziouc saax ziec-dorngh cing-nzengc. Ziec-dorngh ziouc gauh cing-nzengc jiex. 11 Bunh caux bunh nyei taapv yaac oix zuqc zorqv youh dox jienv saax cing-nzengc fungx bun yie, Ziouv.

12 “Dorh Aalon caux ninh nyei dorn taux ndopv-liuh nyei gaengh ndaangc, bun ninh mbuo yiem wuov longc wuom nzaaux sin. 13 Tengx Aalon zuqv jienv zoux sai nyei cing-nzengc lui-houx, aengx zorqv youh dox jienv saax ninh cing-nzengc fungx bun yie, weic zoux sai mienh fu-sux yie. 14 Dorh Aalon nyei dorn mbuo daaih bun ninh mbuo zuqv jienv gu'nyuoz wuov yiemc lui-ndaauv. 15 Zorqv youh dox ninh mbuo hnangv dox ninh mbuo nyei die nor, weic bun ninh mbuo zoux sai mienh fu-sux yie. Hnangv naaiv zoux sai doic jiex doic yietc liuz nzipc jienv mingh.” 16 Ziouv hnangv haaix nor paaiv Mose zoux haaix nyungc, Mose ziouc hnangv wuov nor zoux nyungc-nyungc.

20 Ninh zorqv fiev ziepc diuh Leiz-Latc wuov deix i kuaaiv la'bieiv dapv jienv Laengz Ngaengc Waac Faang gu'nyuoz, zorqv gaeng nyei ndiangx cunx jienv i bung faang ga'hlen nyei kuing, aengx zorqv ceix en nyei weic nyei faang-nqaaix gomv jienv, 21 dorh faang an jienv ndopv-liuh gu'nyuoz, aengx zorqv wuov kuaaiv ndie-mbaeqc mbaeqc jienv torngv Laengz Ngaengc Waac Faang, ziux Ziouv paaiv Mose nyei waac zoux.
