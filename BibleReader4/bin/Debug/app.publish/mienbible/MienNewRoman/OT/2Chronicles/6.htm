Zorqv Zaangc Tin-Hungh Nyei Biauv
Fungx Bun Ninh

(Beiv mangc 1 Hungh Douh 8:12-21 )

1 Wuov zanc Saa^lo^morn daux gaux gorngv, “Ziouv gorngv ninh oix yiem hmuangx haic nyei mbuonx gu'nyuoz. * Fai hmuangx. 2 Yie weic meih ceix ziangx yietc norm nzueic haic nyei biauv, se zoux norm dorngx weic bun meih yietc liuz yiem.”

3 I^saa^laa^en nyei zuangx mienh corc souv jienv wuov, Saa^lo^morn Hungh huin nzuonx daaih buang waac ninh mbuo. 4 Ninh gorngv,

“Ceng Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei Tin-Hungh. Ninh ziux ninh ganh nyei nzuih caux yie nyei die, Ndaawitv, laengz nyei waac longc ninh ganh nyei buoz zoux ziangx. * 2 Saa^mu^en 7:1-13 ; 1 Zunh Doic Douh 17:1-12 Weic zuqc ninh gorngv, 5 ‘Yiem yie dorh yie nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh, cuotv I^yipv daaih, yie maiv ginv haaix fingx I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei haaix norm zingh weic yie nyei Mengh liepc norm biauv. Yie yaac maiv ginv haaix dauh zoux bieiv zeiv dorh yie nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh. 6 Mv baac ih zanc yie ginv Ye^lu^saa^lem weic liepc yie nyei Mengh yiem naaic norm dorngx. Yie yaac ginv Ndaawitv daaih gunv yie nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh.’

7 “Yie nyei die, Ndaawitv, jiez hnyouv oix weic Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei Tin-Hungh nyei Mengh, liepc norm biauv. 8 Mv baac Ziouv gorngv mbuox yie nyei die, Ndaawitv, ‘Meih jiez hnyouv weic yie nyei Mengh liepc norm biauv se longx haic aqv, weic zuqc meih maaih naaiv nyungc za'eix yiem meih nyei hnyouv. 9 Mv baac meih ganh maiv duqv ceix. Se meih nyei cien dorn oix weic yie nyei Mengh ceix norm biauv.’

10 “Ih zanc Ziouv ei ninh laengz nyei waac zoux. Yie nzipc jienv yie nyei die, Ndaawitv, zueiz jienv I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei hungh weic, hnangv Ziouv laengz nor. Yie yaac duqv weic Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei Tin-Hungh nyei Mengh, ceix ziangx biauv. 11 Yie liuc leiz ziangx dorngx an Laengz Ngaengc Waac Faang. Faang gu'nyuoz maaih dongh Ziouv caux I^saa^laa^en Mienh laengz jiez nyei ngaengc waac.”

Saa^lo^morn Daux Gaux

(Beiv mangc 1 Hungh Douh 8:22-53 )

12 Saa^lo^morn ziouc souv jienv Ziouv nyei ziec-dorngh nza'hmien maengx, dorng jienv yietc zuangx gapv zunv nyei I^saa^laa^en Mienh sung buoz daux gaux. 13 (Saa^lo^morn longc dongh siqv zoux yietc norm paangh, ndaauv biaa ndorqc, jangv biaa ndorqc, hlang buo ndorqc. Ninh zorqv paangh an jienv laatc gu'nyuoz, mbu'ndongx nyei dorngx. Ninh faaux naaiv norm paangh dorng jienv yietc zuangx gapv zunv nyei I^saa^laa^en Mienh gueic njiec sung buoz hungx jienv lungh.) 14 Ninh gorngv,

“O Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei Tin-Hungh aac, yiem gu'nguaaic lungh fai ga'ndiev ndau maiv maaih zienh hnangv meih nor. Meih ei jienv meih laengz nyei ngaengc waac bun cing meih ziepc zuoqv nyei hnamv meih nyei bou, dongh longc nzengc hnyouv gan longx meih wuov deix. 15 Meih ei jienv meih nyei bou, yie nyei die, Ndaawitv, laengz nyei waac zoux. Meih ganh nyei nzuih laengz, meih ganh nyei buoz ih hnoi yaac zoux ziangx aqv.

16 “O Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei Tin-Hungh aac, tov ih zanc ei jienv meih caux meih nyei bou, yie nyei die, Ndaawitv, laengz nyei waac zoux. Wuov zanc meih gorngv, ‘Se gorngv meih nyei zeiv-fun faix fim, gan longx yie nyei leiz-latc hnangv meih gan nor, meih ziouc yietc liuz maaih zeiv-fun zueiz
I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei hungh weic.’ * 1 Hungh Douh 2:4 17 O Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en Mienh nyei Tin-Hungh aac, tov meih ih zanc zoux ziangx ziux meih caux meih nyei bou, Ndaawitv, laengz nyei waac.

18 “Tin-Hungh za'gengh oix caux baamh mienh yiem baamh gen fai? Lungh caux gauh hlang jiex nyei lungh, meih yiem maiv njiec. Wuov nyungc, yie ceix naaiv norm biauv meih gauh yiem maiv njiec aqv. * 2 Zunh Doic Douh 2:6 19 O Ziouv, yie nyei Tin-Hungh aac, tov muangx meih nyei bou dorng jienv meih daux gaux jouh nyei waac. Tov muangx meih nyei bou yiem meih nyei nza'hmien daux gaux tov naaiv deix waac. 20 Tov meih hnoi-hnoi muonz-muonz mangc jienv naaiv norm biauv, se dongh meih gorngv naaiv norm dorngx, ‘Yie oix liepc yie nyei Mengh yiem naaiv norm dorngx.’ Meih ziouc muangx haiz meih nyei bou huin naaiv norm dorngx daux gaux nyei waac. * Nzamc Leiz-Latc 12:11 21 Meih nyei bou caux meih nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^
laa^en Mienh, huin naaiv norm dorngx daux gaux nyei ziangh hoc, tov meih yiem wuov lungh, meih yiem nyei dorngx, muangx yie mbuo jouh nyei waac. Muangx haiz liuz yaac tov guangc yie mbuo nyei zuiz.

22 “Se gorngv maaih dauh mienh zoux dorngc ganh dauh mienh ziouc zuqc mienh heuc ndouv zioux, oix zuqc dorh ninh taux naaiv zaangc Tin-Hungh nyei biauv, bun ninh yiem meih nyei ziec-dorngh nza'hmien maengx ndouv zioux. 23 Tov meih yiem gu'nguaaic lungh muangx yaac ei jienv zoux. Tov siemv meih nyei bou mbuo yaac dingc zoux dorngc wuov dauh nyei zuiz, bun ninh ziux ninh zoux nyei zuqc ndaam-dorng, * Faan zaqc Hipv^lu waac gorngv “ndortv ninh ganh nyei m'nqorngv”. aengx dingc maiv zoux dorngc wuov dauh maiv maaih zuiz, bun ninh ziux ninh zoux nyei duqv longx.

24 “Meih nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh, haaix zanc baamz zuiz dorngc meih, ziouc zuqc win-wangv hingh ninh mbuo, nqa'haav ninh mbuo nzuonx daaih lorz meih, yiem naaiv norm biauv nyiemc meih nyei mbuox, daux gaux jouh meih, 25 tov meih yiem gu'nguaaic lungh muangx haiz yaac guangc meih nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh, nyei zuiz, dorh ninh mbuo nzuonx daaih yiem meih ceix bun ninh mbuo caux ninh mbuo nyei ong-taaix nyei deic-bung.

26 “Meih nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh, haaix zanc baamz zuiz dorngc meih, meih ziouc dingc zuiz bun lungh haanz, maiv duih mbiungc njiec, se gorngv ninh mbuo huin naaiv norm dorngx daux gaux, nyiemc meih nyei mbuox yaac guangc ninh mbuo nyei zuiz, 27 tov meih yiem gu'nguaaic lungh muangx haiz ziouc guangc meih nyei bou, se meih nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh, nyei zuiz, yaac njaaux longx nyei jauv heuc ninh mbuo ei jienv zoux. Tov bun mbiungc njiec meih bun meih nyei baeqc fingx nzipc nyei ndau.

28 “Se gorngv yiem naaiv norm deic-bung buangh domh ngorc fai butv domh wuon fai, mbiauh jiex mbuov butv latc douz, fai jopv-nyeic faanv, fai gaeng-mau faanv, fai maaih win-wangv weih gormx oix mborqv meih mbuo nyei haaix norm zingh, fai maiv gunv buangh zuqc haaix nyungc zeqc naanc, haaix nyungc baengc, 29 tov muangx ninh mbuo daux gaux nyei waac. Se gorngv haaix dauh mienh fai meih nyei zuangx baeqc fingx, I^saa^
laa^en Mienh, jouh meih, gorqv-mienh yaac hnyouv mun taux gorqv-mienh nyei zuiz, * Maaih deix sou faan daaih gorngv baengc. yaac sung buoz hungx jienv naaiv norm zaangc Tin-Hungh nyei biauv, 30 tov yiem gu'nguaaic lungh, meih yiem nyei dorngx, muangx haiz yaac guangc ninh mbuo nyei zuiz. Meih yaac hiuv duqv mienh nyei hnyouv, ziux gorqv-mienh zoux nyei jauv tengx ninh mbuo (weic zuqc kungx maaih meih ganh hnangv hiuv duqv zuangx baamh mienh nyei hnyouv). 31 Hnangv naaic weic bun ninh mbuo yiem meih ceix bun yie mbuo nyei ong-taaix nyei deic-bung yietc seix taaih meih yaac gan meih nyei jauv yangh.

32 “Gorngv taux ganh fingx mienh, se maiv zeiz meih nyei I^saa^
laa^en baeqc fingx, mv baac weic meih hlo haic nyei mengh caux meih nyei buoz nyei domh qaqv yiem go nyei deic-bung daaih. Ninh daaih hungx jienv naaiv norm biauv daux gaux, 33 tov meih yiem gu'nguaaic lungh, meih yiem nyei dorngx, muangx haiz yaac ei jienv naaiv dauh ganh fingx mienh tov nyei waac zoux, weic bun lungh ndiev maanc fingx mienh hiuv duqv meih nyei Mengh yaac gamh nziex meih, taaih meih hnangv meih nyei baeqc fingx, I^saa^laa^en Mienh, gamh nziex meih, taaih meih nor. Ninh mbuo yaac haih hiuv duqv yie ceix naaiv norm biauv, mienh ziouc gan meih nyei Mbuox heuc.

34 “Se gorngv meih nyei baeqc fingx cuotv mingh caux ninh mbuo nyei win-wangv mborqv jaax, maiv gunv meih paaiv ninh mbuo mingh haaix norm dorngx, ninh mbuo yaac hungx jienv meih ginv daaih naaiv norm zingh caux yie weic meih nyei Mengh liepc daaih nyei biauv, 35 tov meih yiem gu'nguaaic lungh muangx ninh mbuo daux gaux jouh nyei waac, bun ninh mbuo duqv hingh.

36 “Meih nyei baeqc fingx haaix zanc baamz zuiz dorngc meih, (weic zuqc maiv maaih haaix dauh baamh mienh maiv baamz zuiz), meih ziouc qiex jiez ninh mbuo, zorqv ninh mbuo jiu bun win-wangv caangv ninh mbuo mingh taux win-wangv nyei deic-bung, maiv gunv go fai fatv, 37 se gorngv ninh mbuo yiem zuqc caangv mingh nyei deic-bung jangx zuqc ninh mbuo nyei zuiz ziouc goiv hnyouv goiv eix nzuonx daaih lorz meih gorngv, ‘Yie mbuo baamz zuiz, zoux waaic sic,’ 38 se gorngv ninh mbuo yiem zuqc caangv mingh nyei deic-bung buangv hnyouv buangv eix nzuonx daaih lorz meih, aengx hungx jienv meih jiu bun ninh mbuo nyei ong-taaix nyei deic-bung caux meih ginv daaih nyei zingh caux yie weic meih nyei Mengh ceix daaih nyei biauv daux gaux, 39 tov meih yiem gu'nguaaic lungh, meih yiem nyei dorngx, muangx ninh mbuo daux gaux jouh nyei waac, yaac korv-lienh ninh mbuo. Tov guangc meih nyei mienh nyei zuiz, dongh baamz zuiz dorngc meih wuov deix baeqc fingx.

40 “Tov yie nyei Tin-Hungh ih zanc nungx m'zing mangc jienv yaac baeng m'normh muangx yiem naaiv norm dorngx daux gaux nyei waac.
