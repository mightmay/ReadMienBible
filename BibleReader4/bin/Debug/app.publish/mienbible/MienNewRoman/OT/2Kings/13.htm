Ye^ho^aa^haatc ninh yaac maiv guangc. 3 Weic naaiv Ziouv nouz haic I^saa^laa^en Mienh ziouc zorqv ninh mbuo jiu bun yiem Silie nyei hungh, Haa^saa^en, caux ninh nyei dorn, Mben Haa^ndatc, nyei buoz-ndiev lauh nyei.

4 Ye^ho^aa^haatc njiec qaqv tov Ziouv. I^saa^laa^en Mienh ziouc yiem ninh mbuo ganh nyei biauv hnangv zinh ndaangc nor. * Eix leiz se ninh mbuo taaix-baengh nyei yiem. 6 Mv baac ninh mbuo maiv guangc Ye^lo^mbo^am nyei douh zong baamz nyei zuiz, dongh ninh dorh I^saa^laa^en Mienh baamz nyei zuiz. 18 E^li^saa aengx gorngv, “Zorqv forng daaih maah!” Hungh diex ziouc zorqv daaih.
