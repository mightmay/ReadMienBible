Yo^aapc Gorngv Ndaawitv

1 Maaih mienh mbuox Yo^aapc, “Hungh diex nyiemv yaac nzauh Apc^saa^lom.” 2 Wuov hnoi zuangx baeqc fingx duqv hingh nyei njien-youh tiuv benx nzauh nyiemv nyei jauv weic zuqc wuov hnoi baeqc fingx haiz gorngv, “Hungh diex hnyouv mun, nzauh haic ninh nyei dorn.” 3 Wuov hnoi zuangx mienh sekv-sekv nyei nzuonx daaih bieqc zingh hnangv mborqv jaax bingx jienv biaux, nyaiv haic nyei mienh nor. 4 Hungh diex buang jienv hmien nyiemv mbui nyei gorngv, “O yie nyei dorn, Apc^saa^lom aah! Apc^saa^lom, yie nyei dorn, yie nyei dorn aah!”

5 Yo^aapc bieqc hungh dinc gorngv mbuox hungh diex, “Meih ih hnoi zoux bun meih nyei yietc zungv jien zuqc nyaiv.
