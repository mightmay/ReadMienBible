Daaih buangh Saa^lo^morn wuov zanc ninh zorqv yiem hnyouv hnamv taux nyei nyungc-nyungc mbuox Saa^lo^morn. * Matv^taai 12:42 ; Lugaa 11:31

5 Ninh gorngv mbuox hungh diex, “Yie yiem yie nyei guoqv haiz gorngv taux meih zoux nyei jauv-louc caux meih nyei cong-mengh, za'gengh zien haic aqv. 6 Mv baac yie maiv sienx naaiv deix waac taux yie ganh daaih, yie ganh nyei m'zing yaac duqv buatc.
