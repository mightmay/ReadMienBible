3 Saem^sanx gorngv, “Naaiv nzunc yie za'gengh oix zoux bun Fi^li^saa^die Mienh. 12 Wuov deix mienh gorngv mbuox Saem^sanx, “Yie mbuo daaih ndoh meih, oix zorqv meih jiu bun Fi^li^saa^die Mienh.”
