Oix zuqc muangx Ziouv nyei waac. Nernh Jiex nyei Ziouv, I^saa^laa^en nyei Tin-Hungh, hnangv naaiv gorngv, “Yie oix bun ciouv haic nyei zeqc naanc taux naaiv norm dorngx.
