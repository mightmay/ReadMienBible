14 Laasen caux Le^aa dau Yaakopv, “Yie mbuo nyei die nyei jaa-dingh ih zanc zungv maiv maaih yie mbuo nzipc nyei buonc. 15 Ninh zoux bun yie mbuo hnangv benx ganh fingx mienh nor.
