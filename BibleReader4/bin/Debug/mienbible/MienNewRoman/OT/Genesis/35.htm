Yiem Mbetc^en,
Tin-Hungh Buang Waac Yaakopv

1 Tin-Hungh gorngv mbuox Yaakopv, “Ih zanc meih oix zuqc suiv mingh yiem Mbetc^en. Yiem wuov oix zuqc ceix norm ziec-dorngh weic yie, dongh zinh ndaangc meih biaux meih nyei gorx, Esaau, wuov zanc hinc cuotv daaih bun meih buatc nyei Tin-Hungh.” * Tin Deic Douh 28:11-17

2 Yaakopv ziouc mbuox ninh nyei hmuangv doic aengx caux ninh yiem wuov deix mienh, “Meih mbuo maaih ganh fingx nyei haaix nyungc miuc-fangx oix zuqc guangc taux nzengc. Oix zuqc saax nzengc uix, yienc lui-houx. 3 Mbuo oix zuqc leih nqoi naaiv norm dorngx mingh yiem Mbetc^en. Taux wuov yie oix ceix norm ziec-dorngh weic Tin-Hungh, dongh zinh ndaangc yie buangh zuqc kouv naanc ninh tengx yie, yie mingh taux haaix ninh yaac caux yie mingh, wuov dauh Tin-Hungh.” 4 Ninh mbuo yietc zungv ziouc zorqv ninh mbuo maaih nyei ganh fingx mienh nyei miuc-fangx caux dangh jienv nyei m'normh hiun, jiu bun Yaakopv. Yaakopv ziouc biopv jienv domh mba'ong zuei ndiangx-gorn ndiev, leih Sekem Zingh maiv nangc go. 5 Yaakopv caux gan ninh nyei zuangx mienh cuotv jauv mingh wuov zanc, Tin-Hungh bun huing nzuonx wuov deix zingh nyei mienh gamh nziex haic, ziouc maiv zunc Yaakopv nyei dorn mbuo.

6 Yaakopv caux yietc zungv gan ninh nyei mienh mingh taux Kaa^naa^an Deic-Bung, Lutv Zingh, (se Mbetc^en Zingh.) 7 Taux wuov ninh ceix norm ziec-dorngh daaih aengx cuotv wuov norm dorngx nyei mbuox heuc En Mbetc^en, * En Mbetc^en nyei eix leiz se Mbetc^en nyei Tin-Hungh. weic zuqc biaux ninh nyei gorx, Esaau, wuov zanc, Tin-Hungh yiem wuov hinc cuotv bun ninh buatc.

8 Dongh zinh ndaangc dorh Le^mbe^kaa hlo daaih wuov dauh bou, Nde^mbo^laa, guei seix mi'aqv. Ninh mbuo ziouc zangx jienv yiem Mbetc^en nyei naamh bung, yietc diuh domh mba'ong zuei ndiangx-gorn hlen. Wuov diuh ndiangx ninh mbuo cuotv norm mbuox heuc Anlon Mbaakutv. * Anlon Mbaakutv nyei eix leiz se “nzauh nyiemv nyei mba'ong zuei ndiangx”.

9 Yaakopv yiem Batc^ndaan Aalam Deic nzuonx daaih, Tin-Hungh aengx hinc cuotv nzunc, buang waac bun ninh. 10 Tin-Hungh mbuox ninh, “Meih nyei mbuox heuc Yaakopv mv baac yangh naaiv jiex meih nyei mbuox oix zuqc goiv heuc I^saa^laa^en.” * I^saa^laa^en nyei eix leiz se “caux Tin-Hungh borngz jaax”. I^saa^laa^en. * Tin Deic Douh 32:28

11 Tin-Hungh aengx gorngv, “Yie se Nernh Jiex nyei Tin-Hungh. * Hipv^lu waac gorngv En Satv^ndai. Meih oix zuqc maaih fu'jueiv camv. * Tin Deic Douh 17:4-8 Meih nyei zeiv-fun oix hiaangx daaih benx yietc fingx caux camv-guoqv nyei fingx. 14 Yaakopv zorqv norm la'bieiv don jienv Tin-Hungh caux ninh gorngv waac nyei dorngx, * Tin Deic Douh 28:18-19 yaac zorqv a'ngunc diuv ciev dongc gu'nguaaic, yaac zorqv ga'lanv youh dox jienv. 15 Ninh aengx cuotv wuov norm dorngx nyei mbuox heuc Mbetc^en. * Mbetc^en nyei eix leiz se Tin-Hungh nyei biauv.
