Meih mbuo taux meih mbuo nyei bou naaiv daaih aqv.”

Ninh mbuo dau, “Longx aqv. Gunv ziux meih nyei waac zoux.”

6 Apc^laa^ham gaanv jienv bieqc ndopv-liuh mbuox Saalaa, “Gaanv siepv deix zorqv yietc tongv * Hipv^lu waac gorngv 3 se^aa. Se leih maiv go 22 litv fai yietc tongv. Yietc se^aa se leih maiv go 7.3 litv. mbuonv-muonc caix daaih zoux njuov.”

7 Gorngv liuz, Apc^laa^ham tiux mingh ngongh guanh wuov, zorqv dauh ngongh dorn lunx nyei, junc nyei, bun bou. Bou ziouc gaanv liuc leiz.

8 Apc^laa^ham zorqv deix ngongh nyorx-hmei caux ngongh nyorx aengx caux liuc leiz ziangx nyei ngongh orv mbenc ninh mbuo buo dauh. Nyanc wuov zanc, ninh ganh aeqv, souv jienv ndiangx ga'ndiev, ninh mbuo nyei ga'hlen.

10 Wuov deix buo dauh mienh maaih dauh gorngv, “Da'nyeic hnyangx taux naaiv norm ziangh hoc yie zungv aengx nzuonx taux naaiv. Taux wuov zanc meih nyei auv, Saalaa, ziouc duqv dauh dorn daaih aqv.” * Lomaa 9:9

Saalaa yiem ninh mbuo nqa'haav nqaang ndopv-liuh gaengh ndaangc muangx. 11 Apc^laa^ham mbuo i hmuangv zungv gox haic. Ziouv maiv haih zoux. * Matv^taai 19:26 ; Maako 10:27 ; Lugaa 1:37 ; Lomaa 9:9 Da'nyeic hnyangx taux naaiv norm ziangh hoc yie aengx nzuonx daaih.
