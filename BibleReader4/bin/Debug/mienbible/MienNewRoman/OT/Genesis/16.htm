1 Apc^laam nyei auv, Saalaai, maiv haih yungz fu'jueiv bun Apc^laam. Nziex yie haih laaix ninh duqv fu'jueiv mv bei.”

Apc^laam muangx Saalaai nyei waac. 3 Apc^laam nyei auv, Saalaai, ziouc zorqv ninh nyei nouh beiz wuov dauh I^yipv Mienh, Haagaa, bun ninh nyei nqox longc zoux auv. (Wuov zanc Apc^laam yiem Kaa^naa^an Deic-Bung duqv ziepc hnyangx.) 4 Apc^laam caux Haagaa bueix, Haagaa ziouc maaih jienv sin aqv.
