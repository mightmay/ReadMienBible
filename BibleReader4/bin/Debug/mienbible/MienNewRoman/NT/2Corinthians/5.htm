Haih daic nyei sin ziouc tiuv benx yietc liuz nyei sin. * Gikc waac gorngv “haih daic nyei sin ziouc zuqc yietc liuz nyei sin naqv nzengc”. 5 Tin-Hungh ganh liuc leiz ziangx mbuo, oix bun mbuo hnangv naaiv goiv yienc.
