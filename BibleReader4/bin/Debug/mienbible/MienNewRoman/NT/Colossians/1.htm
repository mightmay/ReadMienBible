Baulo haiz gorngv yiem Ko^lo^si jiu-baang maaih deix zunh jaav leiz nyei fin-saeng njaaux dorngc leiz. Ninh mbuo njaaux, se gorngv oix hiuv duqv Tin-Hungh yaac duqv njoux taux daauh nor, oix zuqc zaangc camv-nyungc yiem ndaamv-lungh maaih lingc maaih qaqv nyei, zoux hlo wuov deix. Yaac oix zuqc jiex gaatv nyei leiz, longx-longx nyei gan nyanc hopv nyei camv-nyungc leiz.

Baulo fiev naaiv zeiv fienx bun Ko^lo^si Zingh nyei jiu-baang hiuv duqv naaiv deix jauv se maiv zeiz zien sienx Yesu nyei jauv. Ninh oix mienh hiuv duqv Yesu Giduc haih njoux mbuo taux daauh taux mueiz. Gan wuov deix camv-nyungc leiz zungv haih dorh mienh dorngc jauv, leih Yesu go. Tin-Hungh longc Giduc zeix lungh zeix ndau yaac oix longc Giduc bun maanc muotc yiem ninh nyei buoz-ndiev benx nzengc ninh nyei. Kungx weic kaux Giduc hnangv baamh gen cingx daaih maaih lamh hnamv.
